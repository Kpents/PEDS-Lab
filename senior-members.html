Awingot Richard Akparibo (PhD)

Lead Researher

Awingot Akparibo is a Lecturer in the Engineering Department of Ashesi University. For the past eight years, he has taught several courses in the Department, including Electrical Machines, Power Electronics, Power Engineering, and Power Systems Analysis. He previously taught Electric Motor Drives at the Regional Maritime University, Nungua, Accra, Ghana.

He also worked as a field operations Engineer at Process and plant automation Ghana Limited, an Electrical Services Engineer at Webb Construction Mining Services Ghana Limited, and a graduate trainee at Anglo Gold Ashanti, Obuasi and the Coca Cola Bottling Company of Ghana Limited. He also provided industrial automation training to students and staff at various Ghanaian institutions, including Sunyani Technical University and Tamale Technical University.

He holds PhD in Electrical Engineering from the University of Cape Town, Western Cape, South Africa, MPhil and BSc degrees both in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from the University of Mines and Technology (UMaT), Tarkwa, Ghana.

Research Interest

  • Power and Energy conversion systems
    • DC-DC
    • DC-AC
    • Renewable Energy Systems
  • Electrical machines
  • Condition Monitoring
