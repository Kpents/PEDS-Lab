PEDS Lab

The Research focus of the PEDS Group revolves around creating cost effective, efficient, and high performing portable Power Electronics platforms for Renewable Energy System monitoring and diagnostics, industrial drive controls and automation of agricultural and health system processes.

The group places strong emphasis on improving system reliability and energy efficiency, through advanced measurement and control techniques.

Additionally, research explores optimizing converter performance, drive dynamics, and energy management strategies across diverse operating conditions. These efforts aim to deliver practical and scalable solutions tailored to the needs of developing and resource-constrained environments.
