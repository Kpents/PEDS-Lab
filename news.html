Latest announcements, publications, and events from the PEDS Lab.

April 20, 2026

PEDS Lab Unveils Tracking and Real-time Characterisation Engine (TRACE) for Real-Time Solar PV Monitoring

The PEDS Lab has developed TRACE (Tracking and Real-time Characterisation Engine), an innovative hardware-software system designed to reconstruct solar PV I–V and P–V characteristic curves in real time
April 20, 2026

PEDS Lab Develops PV-Powered Fine Sand Solar Geyser for Low-Cost Domestic Hot Water Production

The PEDS Lab has developed a project focused on the design, and implementation of a PV-integrated Fine Sand Geyser (PFSG) for domestic hot water production.
March 03, 2026

New publication: Single Stage DC-AC Boost Inverter-Based Perturbation Technique for Online Impedance Spectroscopy Measurements of Photovoltaic Panels

A new publication on studies that show that, with appropriate Simplified Split-Source Boost Inverter (SSSBI) DC-AC topology and Online Impedance Spectroscopy (EIS) signal injection control scheme, a DC-AC inverter can be adopted to provide online EIS estimation of a PV panel for its diagnosis.
